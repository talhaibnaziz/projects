Executive Council

  • Chairman

    Shirin Akhter

    She is a change maker and a national level Advocate. She has a complete masters in English from Dhaka University and works closely with the vulnerable girls and women with disabilities. She is a founding member of WDDF and performs grassroots coordination both nationally and internationally on WDDF activities.

  • Vice-chair

    Masuma Akhter

    Closely involved with the community people to reduce discrimination against girls and women, especially with disabilities. Completed her graduation in Mathematics from Dhaka University. A hard worker transcending multiple forms of personal physical limitations.

  • Member Secretary

    Ashrafun Nahar Misti

    A rights-establishment activist who works hard to incorporate women with disabilities into the national law and policy-making sectors. She has a complete masters in Accounting from National University and works closely with the girls and women with Disabilities both nationally and internationally. She is also involved with the feminist movement, playing the role of executive director in WDDF.

  • Treasurer

    Rounak Jahan Usha

    She became involved with WDDF on 2012 and since then she has been devoted to work with girls and women with Disabilities. She is working in the National Human Rights Commission, Bangladesh, since July 2018. Recently, she became the treasurer of WDDF on July 2018. Although she is a wheelchair user, she has a strong background - to fight for life, to live independently, and to continue her studies. She is a role model to others who are like her.

  • Executive Member

    Sharifa Tarafder Lipi

    She is change maker and mostly involve with humanitarian Action. She is supporting child for their better life. complete masters in general histry from Dhaka Universityand closely work with the vulnerable girls and women with Disabilities Internationally, nationally and do grassroots coordination on WDDF Activities.

  • Executive Member

    Shafia Akhter

    She is an advocate of one of the largest districts of Chittagong. She completed her graduation in humanities from Chittagong University and works closely with the young generation of women with Disabilities. Her interests include journalism.

  • Executive Member

    Sanjida Akhter

    She is a national level Advocate and has studied political science from National University. She is interested in journalism.
